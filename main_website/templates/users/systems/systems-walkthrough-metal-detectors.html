What is Walkthrough/ Handheld Metal Detector Systems?

Walkthrough metal detectors are security screening devices used to detect metallic objects as individuals pass through their sensor frames. Commonly used in high-security areas, these detectors can identify weapons or other metallic contraband without physical searches. They feature adjustable sensitivity levels and multi-zone detection for pinpointing the exact location of metal on a person.

Handheld metal detectors, on the other hand, are portable devices used for close-range scanning. These are ideal for secondary inspections or areas where a walkthrough detector is impractical. Handheld detectors are lightweight, ergonomic, and equipped with audio, visual, and vibration alerts to signal the presence of metal. Together, these tools form a comprehensive security solution for various industries, ensuring quick and efficient screening.
