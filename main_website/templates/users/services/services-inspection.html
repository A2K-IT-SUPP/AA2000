Our Services

AA2000 as your System Inspection Experts

At AA2000 Security and Technology Solution Inc., we provide professional system inspection services to ensure the safety, efficiency, and reliability of your security and technology systems. Regular inspections are essential to identifying potential issues before they become major problems, ensuring that your systems remain fully operational and compliant with industry standards.

Our team of experts conducts thorough inspections of CCTV surveillance, access control, fire detection and alarm systems (FDAS), intercoms, paging systems, turnstiles, and other security solutions. We assess each component’s performance, test system functionalities, check for vulnerabilities, and provide detailed reports with recommendations for maintenance or upgrades.

Whether you're conducting routine inspections, compliance checks, or troubleshooting unexpected system failures, AA2000 ensures that your security infrastructure is always in top condition. We help you avoid costly repairs, minimize downtime, and maintain a secure environment for your business, facility, or home.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing AA2000 means working with highly trained professionals who understand the complexities of security and technology systems. Our team follows industry best practices, utilizing advanced diagnostic tools and testing methods to deliver precise and reliable inspection results.
We provide detailed reports outlining the current state of your system, potential vulnerabilities, and practical recommendations for improvements. Our goal is to ensure that your security systems remain effective, compliant, and fully functional at all times.

Comprehensive Inspection Services

Our system inspection services include:
CCTV System Checks – Ensuring cameras are properly aligned, recording correctly, and functioning at optimal performance.
Access Control Inspections – Verifying biometric scanners, RFID systems, and other access points for proper operation.
Fire Detection and Alarm Testing – Assessing FDAS devices to ensure quick and accurate response to emergencies.
Intercom & Paging System Evaluations – Checking audio clarity, connectivity, and response accuracy.
Network & Power System Assessments – Identifying weak connections, power fluctuations, and backup system reliability. With AA2000, you can have peace of mind knowing that your security and technology systems are in expert hands.
