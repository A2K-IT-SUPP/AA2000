How to Apply?

Contact us via phone, email, or visit our office to discuss your requirements

Joining AA2000 Security and Technology Solution Inc. is a straightforward process designed to ensure we find the right talent for our team. Interested applicants can submit their updated resume and application letter via email or visit our office for in-person submissions. Our hiring team carefully reviews all applications, and shortlisted candidates will go through an evaluation process to determine the best fit for the role.

Candidates who meet the qualifications will be contacted for an initial interview to assess their skills and experience. Depending on the position, additional tests or assessments may be required to evaluate technical capabilities. Once the selection process is complete, successful applicants will receive a formal job offer and begin their onboarding journey with AA2000.

  • Step 1: Submit Your Application – Send your resume and application letter via email or deliver it to our office.

  • Step 2: Initial Screening – Our HR team reviews applications and shortlists candidates based on qualifications.

  • Step 3: Interview Process – Shortlisted applicants are invited for an interview to assess skills, experience, and cultural fit.

  • Step 4: Skills Assessment (If Applicable) – Depending on the role, a technical test or hands-on assessment may be required.

  • Step 5: Final Selection & Job Offer – Successful candidates receive a job offer and proceed with onboarding.
